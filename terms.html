1. Services

Hire Anywhere provides recruitment and headhunting services, placing full-time overseas professionals with UK-based businesses. Our fee is payable only on successful placement, before the candidate's start date.

2. The Integration Guarantee

If a placement does not work out within the guarantee period stated in your engagement document, we will restart the search at no additional charge. This guarantee is void if the candidate's departure is due to redundancy, business closure, or a material change in the role not disclosed at the time of brief.

3. Fees & payment

Our placement fee is calculated as a percentage of the candidate's agreed first-year salary, as specified in your engagement document. Payment is due before the candidate's first day. Invoices are issued in GBP.
